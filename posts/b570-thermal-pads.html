ASRock Arc B570 Thermal Pads Guide

Thermal pad thickness mapping for ASRock Arc B570 PCB and heatsink contact points

This is a guide to thermal pad thickness and a practice for me to write in markdown mode in Vim lol (But needless to say I cannot be held accountable if your B570 breaks).

What’s more interesting is that there is a thermal pad for the 5th VRAM on the heatsink that’s not present on the board. There is a possibility that ASRock may have reused measurements from the B580 model.

Measurement Method

All thermal pad thicknesses were measured and rounded up to the nearest 0.25 mm, as pads compress under mounting pressure.

PCB Side

There are 3 thermal pads contacting inductors on the PCB side, located near the top of the board (connector area).

  • 2 at top side (memory VRM): 1.5mm
  • 1 near 8-pin connector: 1.75mm

Heatsink Side

  • Memory: 1.5mm across
  • Bottom memory VRM: 2mm (drMOS), 1mm (coils)
  • Top memory VRM: 2mm (drMOS)
  • Core VRM: 1.5mm (coils), 1.5mm (drMOS), ~1.75mm uncompressed observed
  • Topside controller: 1.5mm

Comparison Notes

Side-by-side comparison suggests the VRM drMOS pads are closer to 1.75mm uncompressed.

Notes

This is based on physical measurement and visual inspection only. Compression behavior varies depending on mounting pressure, pad material, and heatsink tolerances.

Also collected these measurements as a reference for future repadding or teardown comparison.
